Our Facilities

Library

Our state-of-the-art library provides students with access to a vast collection of academic resources, research papers, and journals for their studies and research work.

Laboratories

Our advanced laboratories are equipped with the latest technology, offering students hands-on experience in various disciplines, including electronics, mechanical, and computer sciences.

Sports Facilities

We provide extensive sports facilities, including a gymnasium, basketball courts, and fields for outdoor activities, promoting physical fitness and teamwork.

Hostel

Our on-campus hostels offer a comfortable and secure environment for students, ensuring they have a homely and conducive atmosphere for studying.

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria offers a variety of healthy and delicious meals at affordable prices, providing students with a space to relax and recharge during their breaks.

Transport

We provide reliable transport services, connecting the campus with various parts of the city, ensuring safe and convenient commuting for students and staff.
